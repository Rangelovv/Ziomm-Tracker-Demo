Injuries
  • Leg - each injury lowers MS by 1/4 of the starting value.
  • Arm - 1st injury lowers all actions with that arm (standard checks are 2-handed) by -2. 2nd injury makes the arm unusable.
  • Head - 1st concussion -1 to all rolls, 2nd = -3 to all rolls, 3rd = fall unconsciouss.
  • Body (if threshold is reached, and TGH 13+ is failed; the effects are halved if successful):
    • Physical (piercing/slashing/blunt): Take 1D6 damage (double on 2nd, triple on 3rd injury in the body)
    • Elemental: Lose 1 Stamina (double on 2nd, triple on 3rd injury in the body) Numbers are always floored.
    Stamina adverse effects
    • Winded (5 Stamina) - reduces all stats by 1
    • Tired (3Stamina) - reduces all stats by 3
    • Fatigued (1Stamina) - reduces all stats by 6
    • Black-out (0 Stamina) - incapacitated for 1 turn. Mandatory rest (or other means, like adrenaline shot) the following turn to recharge stamina.
    Standart components
    • Rock (below 1-z level) - provides a half-cover (blocks legs as target) if the line passes through the component and the defender is positioned in a micro-tile neighboring the tree component (diagonal as well).
    • Rock (1-z level and above) - provides a full LoS block if the line passes through the component.
    • Tree - provides a full LoS block if the line passes through the component and the defender is positioned in a micro-tile neighboring the tree component (diagonal as well).
    • Bush - provides a passive -2 on all ranged attacks against you, and +4 Stealth, when a character is positioned in a micro-tile neighboring the tree component (diagonal as well).
    • Difficult terrain (rocky micro-tiles, window-entry, moving over rocks, etc.) - moving over a difficult terrain micro-tile costs double the MS.
    • Explosive barrel (body modifier) - can be moved (double MS while carrying, or 2-manned for no penalty). If ignited by an attack or a chain reaction, deal HE granade damage.
    • Loot pile/crates - Depending on the size (n) of the loot, roll n D100, each roll corresponds to an item from the armory.
    Climbing
    • Up/down a ladder - normal MS if successful, double if not, fall at 1.
    • Up a climbable wall - double MS if successful, 3AP nothing happens if not.
    • Down a climbable wall - double MS if succesful, fall damage if not
    • Up a vertical wall - not possible without equipment/profs
    • Down a vertical wall - fall damage, reduced if check is succesful
      • only damage at 1, from z-level 1

    A character cannot be left climbing when his initiative ends.

     General proficiency checks

    (STR/DEX) cost 3AP and 0STA; Each check can be done quickly (2AP; -2) or steadily (4AP; +2)

    There could complimentary secondary checks that do not cost AP and are a part of another event (Climbing, Reach, etc.)

    • Lookout - increases passive perception by 5 for the turn (2AP)
    • Hide - increase passive stealth (next to a wall/component) by 5 for the turn (2AP)
    • Take cover - uses half cover or a tree to apply -3 on all ranged attacks against you (2AP) +crouch
    • Loot - 1AP to open, 1 AP to pick-up
    • Change equipment - 1 AP to unequip and 1 to equip, 1 AP to change holding arm
    • Watchout - A character can choose either a line starting from his position, or 3 connected micro-tiles in vision. If an enemy character enters any of the selected micro-tiles, the character who is on the watchout, can use Reaction to attack them.
     Wrestle
    • Melee range, both characters should either unequip their hands, or drop the equipment on the ground. A character might decide to not participate in the wrestle, keeps his weapons and choose to attack afterwards.
    • DEX contest (0AP, attacker has +2).
    • If the defender is "caught", a MMA (+1/2 TGH) contest can be initiated by any of the 2 characters (2,3,4 AP; -2,0,+2 modifier). The contest is scored in the following way:
      • 0-4 - no change
      • 5-9 - +1 contest point
      • 10-14 - +2 contest points
      • 15+ - +3 contest points
    • Each contest point applies -2 to all next contests as well as attacks. 2H weapons are not usable if wrestled.
    • If 5 contest points are reached, the attacker can choose to remain in the wrestle or incapacitate the target for 1 turn. The target has the Fatigued-Tired-Winded states for the next 3 rounds.
    • A character can break the wrestling if contest points are at 0 or he has an advantage. A passive DEX contest is required to break it.
